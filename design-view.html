Design

Colouring Origami Paper and Instruction Kit

This colouring origami book is tailored for children aged 3 to 6, providing them with a delightful combination of colouring and origami activities. The instructions feature various colouring templates, offering multiple options for enjoyment. Blank templates also empower children to unleash their creativity by designing their creations.

As the origami outcomes reveal portions of the paper, these colouring templates have limited colouring areas on the paper. This approach of colouring first before origami encourages them to contemplate how different areas will be showcased in the output, fostering imaginative thinking. A distinctive visual feature of the kit is the bold strokes used to mark the colouring areas in the templates. These strokes are also reflected in the outline strokes on the edges of the package box and the instruction booklet, ensuring visual consistency.

The decision to package the book and paper in a box was made with the target audience, young children, in mind. This choice ensures that individual papers are not lost and are adequately protected. The box design mirroring the origami creations makes audiences anticipate the outcomes of the colouring origami.
