About

Hello there!
I'm Sunmi.

I'm a graphic designer and illustrator based in Winnipeg, Manitoba. I am currently enrolled in the second year of the Graphic Design program at Red River College Polytechnic. Before my studies, I worked in web front-end development for five years. As a result, I am passionate about combining design principles with technology to create innovative solutions.
Art has always been a significant part of my life, serving as a constant source of inspiration. I love exploring arts and experimenting with various mediums. This love for art fuels my dedication to producing visually stunning and impactful designs.
